DEAD BY DAYLIGHT

Death is not an escape. It is only the beginning.

What is Dead by Daylight?

Dead by Daylight is an asymmetrical multiplayer horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive. Released in 2016, it pits four Survivors against one Killer in a deadly game of cat and mouse. Each match is a unique horror experience where teamwork, strategy, and survival instincts are pushed to their absolute limits.

The Entity

All matches take place within The Entity's Realm - a nightmarish dimension created by an ancient, spider-like god that feeds on strong emotions, particularly hope and despair. The Entity abducts people from various times and realities, trapping them in an endless cycle of Trials. Every Generator repaired, every Survivor sacrificed, every moment of terror feeds this cosmic horror.

The Survivors

Four ordinary people thrust into extraordinary circumstances. Survivors must work together to repair five Generators scattered across the map to power the Exit Gates and escape. They cannot fight the Killer directly - only run, hide, and outsmart their pursuer. Communication, resource management, and split-second decisions determine who lives and who gets sacrificed to The Entity.

The Killers

Each Killer is a unique horror archetype with their own tragic backstory and supernatural Power. From vengeful spirits to unstoppable juggernauts, every Killer brings a distinct playstyle that Survivors must adapt to. The Killer's goal is simple: hunt down all four Survivors before they can escape, offering them to The Entity on sacrificial hooks scattered throughout the Trial grounds.

⚡

The Trial Grounds

Matches take place across diverse Realms - each a twisted reflection of a Killer's memories or fears. From the rusted machinery of Autohaven Wreckers to the eerie cornfields of Coldwind Farm, every map is procedurally generated, ensuring no two Trials are exactly alike. Generators, Pallets, Lockers, and Chests spawn in different locations, keeping Survivors on their toes and Killers hunting fresh prey.

The Core Loop

Survivors: Repair Generators → Avoid the Killer → Rescue hooked teammates → Open Exit Gates → Escape.

Killers: Patrol Generators → Find and chase Survivors → Down and hook them → Sacrifice to The Entity → Prevent escapes.

Every match awards Bloodpoints - the universal currency used to progress characters and unlock new abilities.

The Black Fog

The Entity's Realm is shrouded in an unnatural, suffocating fog that limits visibility and heightens paranoia. The Killer's Terror Radius manifests as a heartbeat that grows louder as they approach, warning Survivors of imminent danger. Skilled Killers can manipulate their presence, while clever Survivors learn to read the fog's subtle cues to stay one step ahead of death.

A Living Horror Universe

Dead by Daylight has grown into a massive horror crossover phenomenon, featuring iconic characters from legendary franchises while maintaining its original lore. Regular Chapter releases introduce new Killers, Survivors, and Realms. The game boasts over 60 million players worldwide and continues to evolve with community feedback, seasonal events, and gameplay refinements that keep the horror fresh and terrifying.
